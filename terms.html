Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Select Yun ("we," "our," or "us") operates an automated content publishing application. This Privacy Policy explains how Select Yun handles information when you connect your TikTok account to our service. By using Select Yun, you agree to the terms described herein.

2. Information We Access

Select Yun accesses the following information solely to provide its core functionality:

  • Your TikTok account identifier, used to direct content uploads to your account.
  • OAuth access tokens, used temporarily to authenticate API requests on your behalf.

We do not collect, store, or retain your TikTok credentials, personal profile data, followers, messages, or any content you have not explicitly authorized us to publish.

3. How We Use Information

Information accessed through TikTok's API is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate and authorize automated video uploads on your behalf.
  • Confirm successful publication of content to your TikTok account.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond the above.

4. Data Sharing

Select Yun does not sell, rent, or share your personal information or TikTok account data with any third parties. Access tokens are used only within the scope of TikTok's Content Posting API and are not transmitted to external services.

5. Data Retention

Select Yun does not persistently store OAuth tokens or personal data. Tokens used during an upload session are discarded immediately after use. No user data is retained on our servers beyond the duration of a single upload operation.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect data in transit, including HTTPS encryption for all API communications. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You may revoke Select Yun's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's app settings under "Manage App Permissions." Revoking access will immediately prevent Select Yun from publishing on your behalf.

8. Children's Privacy

Select Yun is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of Select Yun after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
